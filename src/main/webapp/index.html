Introduction

The Food and insulin section will allow you to enter your your blood test results, along with your intake of carbs, reason for test, and any information relating to hypos. It will also allow you to add comments relating to the day to day management of your diabetes.

The activity section will allow you to record your blood sugar before and after activy / exercise. The activitys have been pre-set to golf, running, walking, cycling and badmington.
